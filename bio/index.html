Shaobing Xu is an assistant research scientist (from 1/2019) and was a postdoctoral researcher (10/2016-12/2018) working with Prof. Huei Peng at the Department of Mechanical Engineering (ME) and Mcity, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. He received his Ph.D. degree in Mechanical Engineering from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2016. His work lies at learning, control, and design of autonomous systems with an emphasis on connected automated vehicles (CAVs).
